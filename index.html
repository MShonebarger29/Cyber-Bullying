Cyber Bullying

About

Cyberbullying, a pervasive issue in the digital age, involves the use of electronic communication platforms to harass, intimidate, or harm others. Unlike traditional bullying, cyberbullying transcends physical boundaries, allowing perpetrators to inflict emotional distress from behind the shield of online anonymity. The impact of cyberbullying extends far beyond the digital realm, affecting victims' mental health and well-being. To address this issue effectively, it is imperative for society to promote digital literacy, establish clear policies against cyberbullying, and foster a culture of empathy and responsible online behavior.
